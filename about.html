About this archive

Systems & Society is an independent publication exploring the structures that organise human life. The essays here sit at the intersection of economics, geopolitics, philosophy, psychology, and sociology — not as separate disciplines, but as lenses on the same underlying questions.

The project started from a simple frustration: most commentary moves too fast, chases the news cycle, and mistakes familiarity for understanding. This archive moves slower. Essays are published when they're ready, not on a schedule.

There are no ads, no sponsors, no algorithm to satisfy. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and costs about twelve euros a year — the domain name. Nothing else.

Based in Rotterdam. Written and published in English.
